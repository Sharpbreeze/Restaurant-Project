    OUR STORY

    Our story begins with a Halwai, a six-year-old boy, working in a sweet shop in Rajasthan, India. His name was Judge who worked there to support his family after the passing of his father. While working in the sweet shop, Judge developed a passion for the art of sweet making as well as Indian cuisine.

    Three generations later, and a migration to Canada, today Apna Dhabba is run by the children and grandchildernof Judge Halwai who still can't help but keep a watchful eye well into his "semi-retirement".

    Our talented family members work together to offer our customers fresh, homemade, and mouthwatering sweets and foods that made Judge Halwai popular in his hometown of Hanumangarh, India. Sons passionately prepare food in the kitchen, daughters greet you with a smile, grandchildren tend the tables, and an old friend from India still bakes naan in a clay oven.

    We hope you enjoy your experience and look forward to seeing you soon
